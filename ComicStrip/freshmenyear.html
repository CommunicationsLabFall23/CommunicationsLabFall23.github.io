Freshman Year

Noora arrives at NYUAD, amidst two heavy luggaes and confusing dorm locations, Noora spots an enthusiastic Rija, an upperclasssman, who comes to Noora, literally and symbolically, to her rescue

Noora meets her roommates, Tasnim, an Emirati who's deeply passionate about environmental conservation; Maimuna, an Indian tech whiz; and Nusra, an Ethopian filmmaker. Initial awkwardness, cultural blunders, and the joy of discovering common interests, will they stay friends or the freshmen housing chaos will dawn on them.

Noora is introduced to the bundle of opportunities on campus, from multiple multimodal majors to student clubs, what will Noora pick? How many can she pick?
